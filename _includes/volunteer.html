Teach a Child!

We are looking for volunteers to help us teach our kids programming in the CWB Scratch Code Camp. You will be paired with a child from a secondary school in an underserved area and tutor them for five consecutive weekends - starting from the 5th of november. You will be trained for this programme, all we require from you is to have a laptop and your time (a couple of hours) on weekends. You will get a certificate acknowledging you volunteered, but most importantly, you will have fun, while being a part of a child's future.
